About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Boppys, the heart and soul of finger-licking chicken across the North West! At Boppys, we’ve turned the simple love of chicken into an art form, serving up crispy, juicy, and irresistibly seasoned meals that have people coming back for more. From our humble beginnings to becoming a beloved local favorite, Boppys is more than just a chicken shop—it’s a place where community, quality, and flavor meet.

Our Story

Boppys started with a simple idea: to bring people together over great-tasting chicken. Founded by food enthusiasts with a passion for bold flavors, we’ve grown from a single corner shop into a thriving chain across the North West. Every Boppys location carries the same mission: to create a welcoming space where friends, families, and chicken lovers alike can enjoy meals that feel homemade but with a twist that’s uniquely ours.

Our Chicken

At Boppys, quality is non-negotiable. We source only the freshest, responsibly raised chicken and prepare it with our secret spice blends that deliver a perfect balance of crunch and flavor in every bite. Whether it’s our classic fried chicken, our signature wings, or our mouthwatering burgers, every item on our menu is crafted with care, ensuring every meal is memorable.

Our Values

We believe in more than just great food. At Boppys, we pride ourselves on:

  • Community First: Supporting local suppliers, giving back to neighbourhoods, and creating spaces where everyone feels welcome.
  • Passion for Quality: Every piece of chicken is prepared with attention to detail, from marination to frying to plating.
  • Innovation: We’re constantly experimenting with new flavors, sauces, and sides to keep your taste buds excited.

Why The North West Loves Us

From bustling city streets to quieter towns, Boppys has become synonymous with fun, flavourful dining. Whether it’s a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a late-night craving, we’re here to deliver smiles—and, of course, unforgettable chicken.

So next time you’re in the North West, come and experience Boppys — where every bite tells a story, and every meal is a celebration of flavour, community, and good times.
